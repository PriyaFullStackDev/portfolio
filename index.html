ABOUT US

Nurturing Young Minds Since 2010

Jumbo Kids Playschool is a premier early childhood education center dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive.

Our play-based curriculum is designed to foster creativity, curiosity, and confidence in young learners, preparing them for a lifetime of success.

OUR PROGRAMS

Age-Appropriate Learning Programs

We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the developmental needs of children at different ages and stages.

1.5 - 2.5 Years

Playgroup

Introduction to school environment through play and fun activities.

2.5 - 3.5 Years

Nursery

Developing social skills and early learning concepts through interactive play.

3.5 - 5.5 Years

Kindergarten

Preparing for formal schooling with structured learning and activities.
